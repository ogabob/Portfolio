Robert Ruff Grant for Math Majors

This is a grant for Math Majors . To qualify, the must be enrolled in any college in the United States and must have a GPA of at least 3.5 at the time of application. If you you qualify, the grant will last for a year after which the student GPA will be evalauted. As long as the student keeps the GPA of 3.5 and above, student will receive the grant through out the academic program. 12 Students will be picked every year after an interview and student academic perfomance evaluation. Robert Ruff is an equal opportunity grant awarder...
