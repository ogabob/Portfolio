Walk2Learn International Facts

Walk2Learn International has donated over 100,000 books to schools, libraries and orphanages in Ghana.

Walk2learn International Disability Initiative (WIDI) has donated over 10,000 Braille books and resources to the blind.

W2LI has instituted afterschool reading clubs in reading, math and science in all the schools that we donate books to.

Clinic on Wheels [C.O.W] ™ program, provides access to primary health care services to the underserved population from region to region.

Clinic in A Box [C.A.B] ™ program to build a multi-faceted first aid clinic to provide the underserved communities with the basic medical care needed.

The“SAVE”™ program provides prenatal care to mother and child, and neonatal service to children-under 5 in underprivileged communities.
