Book Donations

Shipping of books is the central part of our organization. Books collected from different sources are sorted and packed by volunteers and are shipped in pallets or sea containers that hold 20,000 to 25,000 units. It costs from $1,500 to $5,000 to send pallets of books toAfrica. You can sponsor a shipment of books to Ghana or other parts of Africa.If you would like to donate to a specific shipment please contact us at
walk2learninternational@gmail.com
